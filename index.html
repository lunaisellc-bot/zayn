About ZAYN

We design every piece in-house. Balanced proportions, modern lines and thoughtful finishing. Our fabrics are high-grade polyester: durable, color-fast and drapes beautifully. Production is on-demand — this avoids waste and lets us keep quality high.

Our Craft

Cut, sewn and finished in small runs. Seams are tidy; prints are sharp. A clean look that lasts.

Why Us?

Refined, never loud. Elevated basics that layer well and feel considered — day to night.

Our Story

ZAYN began with a simple question: *what stays beautiful when trends pass?* We design with restraint—clean lines, architectural shapes and a palette that lets form speak. Each piece embraces everyday ease while honoring runway discipline.

We are proudly based in Wyoming, crafting small-batch capsules to keep quality high and waste low. Collections are intentionally compact, thoughtful and timeless.

Milestones
  • 2023 — First capsule: *Monochrome Lines*
  • 2024 — Launch on Etsy & international shipping
  • 2025 — Editorial expansion: outerwear & tailored essentials

Aesthetic & Feelings

Our aesthetic lives between **calm** and **confidence**—quiet luxury. Fabrics drape, seams align, proportions balance. The silhouette does the talking.

We aim for pieces that feel like a deep breath: light, composed, reliable. Style that ages beautifully.

Design Pillars
  • Minimal • Architectural • Editorial
  • Neutral monochromes with subtle depth
  • Texture > prints, form > noise

Materials & Craft

We partner with vetted mills and workshops, selecting fabrics for **hand-feel, drape and longevity**. Many pieces are finished by hand for precision.

Care Principles
  • Prefer gentle wash & air dry
  • Steam to refresh silhouette
  • Store on wide hangers to keep structure
